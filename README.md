End-to-end data analysis using MySQL and Power BI to identify a ₹68 Lakh revenue drain risk.
#Project Overview
This project analyzes employee attrition data to identify the high-risk segments and to quantify the financial impact on the organization. By migrating rawdata from 'Excel' to 'MySQL' and visualizing insights in 'Power BI'. A critical ₹68,07,246 annual replacement risk was identified.

#Key Business Insights
- The Financial Drain: Monthly revenue loss stands at '₹11.34 Lakh'.
- Primary Risk Segment: Young Talent (Ages25-34).
- The Burnout Factor: 73 employees with a 'far commute' of 15+km signifies the highest immediate flight risk.
- Academic Impact: Life Sciences and Medical backgrounds account for over 60% of total attrition.

#Strategic Recommendations
1. Mitigate Commute Burnout: Based on the 73 attrition due to far commute the organization must implement hybrid policies for the remaining high distance workforce to prevent further workforce loss and revenue drain.
2. 2.Incentive Alignment: Review compensation for mid-career staff to protect senior-level revenue.
3. 3.Retention: With the sales department accounting for 38% attrition and 44.5% of monthly revenue drain, prioritize a 'one quarter retention audit' to stabilize the core revenue engine.
